A report accusing Huawei of having once had "unlimited access" to phone calls made using one of the Netherlands' leading operators, KPN, has been published by a Dutch newspaper. The report was compiled by the CapGemini consultancy in 2010 but never released. Those affected would have included former Prime Minister Jan Peter Balkenende. The Chinese technology giant has completely denied the allegations.

All 50 states, Washington D.C. and Puerto Rico have expanded coronavirus vaccination eligibility to all adults, meeting President Biden’s deadline. The administration is now turning to persuading more Americans to get vaccinated.

The British government said it would investigate Nvidia’s $40 billion deal to buy British chip designer Arm from SoftBank, widening the regulatory scrutiny of the proposed transaction.
